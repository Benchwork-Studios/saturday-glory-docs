# Join Codes & Team Assignment

## How coaches join

Every league has a **join code** — a short alphanumeric string coaches enter on the web portal to register. The join code is visible in the League Hub under **Server Status**.

To copy it: click the copy icon next to the code. Share it with coaches however you prefer — text, email, Discord, etc.

Coaches navigate to your server URL in any browser, enter the join code and a display name, and they're in. They receive a **session token** at that point — this is their identity. They should save it somewhere safe (the portal shows it once after joining) in case they need to reconnect from a different browser.

## Assigning teams

Coaches who join appear immediately in the **Connected Coaches** section. Until you assign them a team, they can see the portal but won't have a team page or appear in the Submissions tracker.

To assign a team:

1. Find the coach in the Connected Coaches list
2. Click the team name under their display name (shows **Unassigned** if not yet assigned)
3. A dropdown appears with all FBS teams grouped by conference
4. Select a team — teams already claimed by another coach show **(Taken)** and cannot be selected

The assignment takes effect immediately. The coach's portal updates to show their team.

## Reassigning and unassigning

You can change a team assignment at any time:

- **Reassign:** Click the current team name and select a different team
- **Unassign:** Click the current team name and select **Unassign** (shown in red at the top of the menu)

Unassigning a coach does not remove them from the league — they remain connected but lose their team page until reassigned.

## What coaches see after joining

After a coach joins and is assigned a team, their portal shows:

- Their team's roster and depth chart
- The current game plan submission form
- Their recruiting board and available actions
- League standings and schedule
- The league chat

Coaches who have joined but have no team assigned see a placeholder until you assign them.

## Generating a new join code

The join code resets automatically when you **Reset League**. There is no way to rotate the join code without a full reset. If you want to prevent new coaches from joining mid-season, you don't need to do anything — coaches who don't have the code cannot join.
