# Deadlines

Deadlines are optional. They give coaches a visible target date and time for submitting their game plan. The server does not auto-advance when a deadline passes — advancing is always a manual commissioner action.

## Setting a deadline

1. In **League Hub → Season Control**, tap **Set Advance Deadline**
2. A date and time picker appears
3. Select the date and time, then tap **Set**

The deadline is stored on the server and visible to coaches in the portal. It appears as a countdown on their dashboard.

## Changing a deadline

Tap **Change Deadline** in Season Control — the same picker appears with the current deadline pre-filled. Adjust and tap **Set**.

## Clearing a deadline

Tap **Clear** next to the displayed deadline. The deadline is removed immediately and coaches no longer see a countdown.

## What happens when the deadline passes

Nothing automatic. The server records that the deadline has passed and the coach portal reflects it, but the league does not advance on its own. You still need to:

1. Check the Submissions section for who has and hasn't submitted
2. Lock submissions
3. Advance the week manually in the Mac app

This keeps you in control — if a coach has a valid reason for being late, you can wait. If you're ready to move on, you advance.

## Recommended cadence

There's no required schedule, but a common approach for async leagues:

| Day | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Monday | Advance the previous week, unlock submissions |
| Thursday | Set deadline for end of day |
| Friday | Lock submissions and advance |

Adjust to fit your group's availability. The server has no concept of real-world time outside of the deadline display.
