# Transfer Portal

The transfer portal opens during the offseason and allows college players to move between programs with immediate eligibility.

## Portal window

The portal window runs for **8 weeks** during the offseason — shorter than the recruiting contact period. Coaches pursue portal players alongside regular recruiting during this window.

## How players enter the portal

**Low playing time** — Sophomore, junior, and senior players who are third-string or lower have a **15% chance** of entering the portal each offseason.

**Random departures** — Any player has a **2% chance** of entering the portal regardless of playing time.

Each team generates a maximum of **3 portal entries** per offseason cycle.

## Pursuing portal players

Portal players appear on a separate board. Interest mechanics work the same as recruiting — spend resources to raise a player's interest in your program. Portal players decide faster because the window is shorter.

Minimum commit interest for portal players: **25** (higher than recruiting's 20 — experienced players are more selective).

AI teams target **10 portal players** per offseason.

## Immediate eligibility

All portal transfers in Saturday Glory are immediately eligible. No sit-out year. A player who commits through the portal is available from Week 1 of the next season.

## Portal vs. recruiting

| | Recruiting | Transfer Portal |
|-|-----------|----------------|
| Window | 12 weeks | 8 weeks |
| Minimum commit interest | 20 | 25 |
| AI target list | 25 per team | 10 per team |
| Eligibility | Freshman year | Immediate |
| Player type | High school prospects | College players |
