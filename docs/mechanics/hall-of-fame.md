# Hall of Fame

The Saturday Glory Hall of Fame recognizes players and coaches whose careers meet the criteria for induction. It builds over multiple seasons as a historical record of your simulation's most accomplished figures.

## Player induction criteria

Players are evaluated at the end of their final season based on:

- **Overall rating** — high career ratings at premium positions
- **Career longevity** — sustained contributions across multiple seasons
- **Statistical production** — standout career numbers at their position
- **Championship contributions** — conference titles, CFP appearances, bowl wins

Players who meet the threshold are inducted automatically — no manual selection required.

## Coach induction criteria

Coaches are evaluated at career end (retirement or final firing) based on:

- **Career win total** — wins above the induction threshold
- **Bowl appearances and wins**
- **Conference championships**
- **National championships** — CFP final victories
- **Coach of the Year awards** — recognition from the simulation's award system

## Viewing the Hall of Fame

The Hall of Fame is accessible within the Mac app and grows each season as new figures qualify. It provides a long-term historical view of your simulation's greatest careers.

## Hall of Fame and recruiting

Programs with a strong Hall of Fame history benefit in recruiting. Recruits with a **Prestige** preference are drawn to programs with decorated legacies — the Hall of Fame is part of what builds that reputation over time.
