# Recruiting System

Recruiting is how programs build their rosters with incoming freshman talent. The contact period runs 12 weeks; coaches spend Action Points to raise interest before Signing Day.

## Contact period

The recruiting contact period runs **12 weeks**, overlapping the regular season and offseason. Coaches receive **50 base Action Points per week** (modified by the head coach's recruiting rating). AP resets weekly — unspent AP does not carry over.

## Actions and interest

Spending AP raises a recruit's interest score (0–100). A recruit commits on Signing Day when their interest meets the minimum threshold and your program is their top choice.

| Action | AP Cost | Base Interest Gain | Repeatable? |
|--------|---------|-------------------|-------------|
| Phone Call | 5 | +4.0 | Yes — weekly |
| Scout | 10 | +6.0 | No — one time |
| Offer Scholarship | 15 | +8.0 | No — one time |
| Campus Visit | 20 | +12.0 | No — one time |
| Home Visit | 25 | +16.0 | No — one time |

Scouting reveals the recruit's overall rating — without it, their rating shows as "?".

## Recruit preferences

Each recruit has a primary preference that amplifies interest gains when your program matches:

| Preference | Favors |
|-----------|--------|
| **Prestige** | Higher-prestige programs (1–100 scale) |
| **Academics** | Higher academic rating |
| **Playing Time** | Programs where the recruit will see the field sooner |
| **Proximity to Home** | Programs in the same recruiting region |
| **Coaching Staff** | Head coach's recruiting and development ratings |

A prestige-focused 5-star prospect responds more strongly to elite programs even at the same AP spend.

## Signing Day

At the end of the contact period, each recruit commits to their top program based on:

- Total interest accumulated (your AP investment)
- Program prestige
- Depth at their position
- Preference alignment

Minimum commit interest: **20**. AI programs recruit simultaneously — if multiple teams pursue the same recruit, the one with the highest interest wins.

## AI recruiting

AI teams maintain a target list of **25 recruits per class** and spend AP automatically each week, competing against you and each other.
