# Game Plan

The Game Plan page is where you set your team's offensive and defensive tendencies for the upcoming game. You submit once per week during the regular season, conference championships, and bowl games.

## What you're setting

| Setting | What it controls |
|---------|-----------------|
| **Run / Pass Ratio** | The percentage of offensive plays that are run vs. pass. Slider from 0% (all pass) to 100% (all run). Default is 50/50. |
| **Base Defense** | Your primary defensive formation: 4-3, 3-4, Nickel, or Dime. |
| **Blitz Frequency** | How often your defense sends extra rushers. 0–100%. |
| **Coverage Preference** | Zone or Man coverage behind your pass rush. |

## How to submit

1. Navigate to **Game Plan** in the nav bar
2. Adjust the sliders and dropdowns to match your strategy
3. Click **Submit Game Plan**

After submitting, the page shows a summary of your submitted plan and a message that you're waiting on the commissioner to advance. The form is replaced with a read-only summary until the next week.

## Locks and re-submission

When the commissioner **locks submissions**, the game plan form is disabled. A banner appears at the top of the page. You cannot change your plan after it's locked.

If you haven't submitted before the lock, your last submitted plan (or the default 50/50 settings) will be used in the simulation.

When the commissioner advances the week, the game plan form resets for the new week.

## When game plans are available

Game plans can be submitted during:
- Regular season weeks
- Conference championship week (if your team qualifies)
- Bowl season (if your team earned a bowl bid)

During the **offseason**, **spring practice**, and **fall camp** phases, the game plan form is not available — focus on recruiting and roster management instead.

## Marking yourself Ready

After submitting your game plan and taking your recruiting actions, click **I'm Ready** on your dashboard. This tells the commissioner you're done for the week. The commissioner can see each coach's ready status in the League Hub.

You can still change your game plan after marking Ready, as long as submissions aren't locked.
