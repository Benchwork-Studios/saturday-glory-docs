# Joining a League

## What you need

- The **server URL** from your commissioner (e.g. `https://league.yourdomain.com`)
- The **join code** from your commissioner — a 6-character alphanumeric code (e.g. `AB3X9Z`)

## How to join

1. Open the server URL in any browser
2. Enter the join code in the **Join Code** field — it auto-capitalizes as you type
3. Enter your name in the **Your Name** field — this is how you appear in the League Hub and chat
4. Click **Join League**

After joining you're taken to a **token save screen**. Don't skip past it — see [Your Session Token →](./your-token).

## After joining

Once the commissioner assigns you a team, your dashboard shows your team name, record, next opponent, and the current phase callout with links to the relevant actions for that phase.

If you see "Team not yet assigned," your commissioner will assign you a team before the season starts. No action needed from you.

## Common errors

| Error | What it means |
|-------|--------------|
| "Invalid join code" | The code is wrong, or the league was reset and has a new code. Ask your commissioner. |
| "Too many attempts" | 5 failed attempts in one minute triggers a rate limit. Wait a minute and try again. |
| "Could not reach the server" | The server may be down or the URL is wrong. Check with your commissioner. |

## Rejoining from a new browser

Your session is stored locally in the browser. A different browser or device won't have your session. Use the **Restore Session** link on the join page and paste your saved token to reconnect. See [Your Session Token →](./your-token).
