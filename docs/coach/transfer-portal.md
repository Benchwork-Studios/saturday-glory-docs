# Transfer Portal

The transfer portal allows players to move between programs between seasons. As a coach, you can both pursue incoming transfers and have players on your roster enter the portal.

## How the transfer portal works

During the offseason, eligible players from other programs may enter the transfer portal. Portal players are immediately eligible at their new school — no sit-out year required.

The transfer portal cycle runs during the offseason phase, overlapping with recruiting. You have a limited number of portal pursuits each offseason in addition to your regular recruiting AP.

## Pursuing transfer players

Portal players appear on a separate board during the offseason. Like recruiting, you spend resources to express interest and extend offers. Portal players tend to commit faster than high school recruits — the portal period is shorter.

Factors that affect a portal player's decision:
- **Program prestige** — higher-prestige programs have a natural advantage
- **Position need** — players prefer programs where they'll see playing time
- **Interest built** — actions you take during the portal window

## Players entering the portal from your roster

Players who are dissatisfied — due to lack of playing time, program performance, or other factors — may enter the transfer portal from your roster. This is managed by the simulation engine and commissioner. If a player enters the portal from your team, you'll see their status change on the roster page.

You cannot prevent a player from entering the portal once they've decided to leave.

## Immediate eligibility

All transfer portal players in Saturday Glory are immediately eligible — there is no transfer waiver process. A player who commits to your program during the portal window is available to play from Week 1 of the following season.

## Portal vs. recruiting

| | Recruiting | Transfer Portal |
|-|-----------|----------------|
| **Who's available** | High school prospects | College players from other programs |
| **Timeline** | Throughout the season and offseason | Offseason only |
| **Decision speed** | Slower — Signing Day commitment | Faster — shorter portal window |
| **Eligibility** | First-year players (FR) | Immediate eligibility |
