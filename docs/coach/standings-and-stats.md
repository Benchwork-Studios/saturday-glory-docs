# Standings & Stats

## Standings page

The Standings page shows current conference standings for all FBS conferences. Each conference table shows:

- **Team name**
- **Conference record** (W-L within conference games)
- **Overall record** (W-L across all games)

Conference standings determine which teams advance to their conference championship game. Division winners (in conferences with divisions) face off in the title game.

## Schedule page

The Schedule page shows your team's full season schedule — past and upcoming games:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Week** | The week number in the season |
| **Opponent** | Who you're playing |
| **Home/Away** | Whether the game is at your stadium or theirs |
| **Game type** | Regular season, conference championship, bowl game |
| **Result** | Score and W/L for completed games |

Upcoming games show the opponent and location. Completed games show the final score.

## Dashboard summary

Your **Dashboard** gives you a quick-glance summary without navigating to the full schedule or standings:

- **Record** — your current overall win-loss record
- **Next Game** — upcoming opponent, week number, and home/away status
- **Last Result** — result and opponent from your most recent game

The dashboard also shows a real-time result notification when your game is simulated — a banner appears briefly with the score when the commissioner runs that week's simulation.

## Rankings

The **Rankings** page (accessible from the nav bar) shows the current AP poll — a ranking of the top 25 teams in the country based on the simulation's poll calculation. Rankings update after each week is simulated.

Being ranked affects bowl eligibility, recruiting interest, and program prestige over time. Winning games against ranked opponents carries extra weight in the poll.
