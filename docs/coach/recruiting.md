# Recruiting

The Recruiting Board is where you build your roster for future seasons. Each week during recruiting periods, you spend **Action Points (AP)** to pursue incoming prospects.

## The prospect board

The recruiting board lists all recruits in the current class. Each prospect shows:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Name** | Prospect's name |
| **Stars** | Rating from 2–5 stars (5 is highest) |
| **Pos** | Position (QB, RB, WR, etc.) |
| **Hometown** | Where the prospect is from |
| **OVR** | Scouted overall rating (revealed after scouting) |
| **Interest** | How interested the prospect is in your program |
| **Actions** | Buttons to spend AP on this recruit |

## Interest tiers

A prospect's interest in your program is tracked as a tier:

| Tier | What it means |
|------|--------------|
| **Hot** | Very interested — high chance of committing |
| **Warm** | Interested — worth pursuing |
| **Lukewarm** | Mildly interested — needs more attention |
| **Cold** | Not interested — requires significant investment |
| **None** | No contact made yet |

## Action Points

You have a limited pool of Action Points each recruiting week. Spending AP on a recruit increases their interest in your program. The AP bar at the top of the page shows how many points you have remaining.

## Recruiting actions

| Action | AP Cost | Effect |
|--------|---------|--------|
| **Call** | 5 AP | Light contact — small interest boost |
| **Scout** | 10 AP | Reveals the prospect's scouted overall rating |
| **Offer** | 15 AP | Extends a scholarship offer — significant interest boost |
| **Campus Visit** | 20 AP | Brings the recruit to campus — large interest boost |
| **Home Visit** | 25 AP | Visit the recruit at home — largest single-week boost |

Each action can only be used **once per recruit per week**. Used actions are disabled in the table.

## Filters

Use the filter bar to narrow the prospect board:
- **Position** — show only recruits at a specific position
- **Stars** — filter by star rating
- **Interest** — filter by interest tier, or show only prospects you've offered

## Signing Day

At the end of the recruiting period, prospects decide where to commit based on accumulated interest, program prestige, and position need. Recruits who committed to your program are added to your roster for the next season.
